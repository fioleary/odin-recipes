Eggplant Parm

How to Make Eggplant Parm

Eggplant Parm is usually too oily, but with this no fry version, you get all the falvor with none of fat. And it's simple, only requireing a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggplants
  • 1 Jar Marinara Suace
  • Shredded Mozzerella Cheese
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 2 Eggs

Steps

  1. Cut the eggplant into sliced
  2. For every slice dip into egg and breadcrumbs
  3. bake
  4. Layer ingredients
  5. bake again
